Ron Forster

← Back to Our Team

Partner - B.Comm, CPA, CA

Over his 40 years of practice Ron has serviced clients in a variety of industries, concentrating in the provision of services to owner managed businesses. This involves many facets including deciding whether to incorporate, succession of business to family members, assistance with financing, exit strategies, and wills and estate planning.

Over the last years, Ron's practice continues to grow in the areas of real estate, construction and not-for-profit organizations (including sitting on the Board of Directors). He provides a wide range of services with a strong emphasis on taxation, auditing and consulting and takes special pride on his long term relationships with his clients.

Ron received his undergraduate degree from McGill University in 1979 and his CA designation in 1981. He is currently a member of CPA Canada and CPA Ontario.
