Associate - BBA, CPA, CA

Michael received his Bachelor of Business Administration from York University in 1978 and his CA designation in 1980. After spending four years at Price Waterhouse, Michael joined with his father, Abraham, where he worked with him until his retirement in 1991. The practice continued under Michael until 2017 when he became an associate of Ron’s firm.

Over the forty years in practice, Michael has worked with owner managers of small to medium sized businesses in a plethora of different industries and now focuses on personal tax planning and estates and trusts.

In his spare time, Michael enjoys the quality time spent with clients and friends on the golf course and the challenge of handicapping thoroughbred races.
