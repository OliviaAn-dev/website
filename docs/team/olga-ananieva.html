Partner - CPA

Olga joined the firm in January 2012 as a co-op student and became a full-time staff member in 2013. Her primary responsibilities are preparation of financial statements and tax returns, both corporate and personal. She also assists clients with payroll and remittance requirements and handles audits of many of the firm’s not-for-profit organizations.

In 2013 Olga graduated with an Advanced Diploma in Accounting and Finance with Honours from Seneca College, and in 2017 has successfully attained Honours Bachelors of Commerce from Laurentian University. In 2018, Olga passed her final examinations for the Chartered Professional Accountant certification program.
