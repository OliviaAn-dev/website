Partner - CPA, CGA

John started with the firm in January of 2006 as a co-op student before joining on a full-time basis in 2008. John focuses on the preparation of financial statements for corporations and charitable organizations and is proficient in the preparation of personal tax returns. In addition, John is part of our client payroll services and is very active in dealing with CRA as it relates to disputes over tax filings.

In December 2007 John graduated from Seneca College with an Advanced Diploma in Accounting and Finance. In June 2015 John graduated from Laurentian University with an Honours Bachelors of Commerce. Subsequently, in November, 2015 John qualified as a Certified General Accountant (CGA) and Chartered Professional Accountant.

John is a member of CPA Ontario and CPA Canada.
