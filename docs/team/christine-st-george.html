Supervisor

Christine joined the accounting practise in 2000. Christine's main focus has been working on owner managed businesses in a variety of industries. Over the years, Christine has gained great knowledge and expertise in QuickBooks accounting software and provides clients with assistance in the setup, training clients on how to use QuickBooks, and assisting clients in resolving problems with their financial records. Christine works directly with the partners in the preparation of financial statements, corporate and personal tax returns, HST, payroll and other compliance issues. In addition, Christine is instrumental in implementing and problem solving new computer software initiatives and programs.

Christine received her Business Administration - Accounting diploma with Honors from Centennial College in 2001.
